SensePi

Sense Pi is a low-cost, low-power motion sensor that can configured for various scenario with a mobile app.

Super low power

Continuous sensing for over a year on two AA batteries

Simple to configure

Use our Android and iOS mobile app to configure the various parameters for the different modes to make it work exactly as you need

Swappable lens

Choose either the narrow-angle long range lens or the wide-angle lens according to your application
Narrow lens: 22m max range & 6 degree FoV
Wide lens: 12m max range & 90 degree FoV

Environment proof

Use it in rain or shine, SensePi can handle the outdoor environment

About

Appiko is an early stage start-up focused on developing technology for wildlife conservation. The first product developed is SensePi, with SenseBe and SenseAu on the way.

Free & open source design

Every part of the developed technology is and will be open. For wildlife conservation, the more competitors, copiers and collaborators, the better. Our repositories are both on Gitlab and Github.

State of the art

There is little-sustained seepage of cutting edge technology into wildlife conservation and research. Appiko aims to change that.
