Appiko

Technology for wildlife conservation

About

Appiko is an early stage start-up focused on developing technology for wildlife conservation. The first product being developed is a wireless DSLR triggering system.

Free & open source design

Every part of the developed technology is and will be open. For wildlife conservation, the more competitors, copiers and collaborators, the better.

State of the art

There is little-sustained seepage of cutting edge technology into wildlife conservation and research. Appiko aims to change that.
